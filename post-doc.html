Post-Doc Support


A postdoctoral position, or postdoc, is a temporary research role undertaken after earning a PhD to gain advanced training and experience in a specific field. It involves conducting research under the mentorship of a senior scientist to prepare for an academic or research-intensive career. We support candidates pursuing postdoctoral positions at world-renowned universities under the guidance of distinguished professors. To date, nearly 40 plus candidates have completed their postdoc at various institutions, including IUH Vietnam, University of Louisiana USA, and University of South Florida. Currently, candidates are conducting their research at SIT Singapore and NKUST Taiwan.

In addition to research support, we offer career development and networking assistance to help you chart a successful career path beyond your post-doc appointment. Our team provides guidance on job search strategies, CV/resume writing, interview preparation, and networking opportunities. We also facilitate connections with potential collaborators, mentors, and industry partners to expand your professional network and enhance your career prospects. Whether you're pursuing academic positions, industry roles, or entrepreneurial ventures, our support services are tailored to help you achieve your career goals.
